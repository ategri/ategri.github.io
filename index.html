Providing specialist biometric systems to address unconventional border control challenges

Ategri was founded to support customers that are underserved by the industry due to their unusual requirements, challenging landscape, or unique geo-political situation.

Today, Ategri BioBMS delivers a modern, efficient, accurate border management system that brings the latest technologies to the front line - in a highly-usable, flexible, cost-effective package.

Extensive functionality out-of-the-box

Preconfigured Entry / Exit workflows

Paper and Electronic Travel Document (MRTD) handling

Face / Fingerprint / Iris biometric matching

Persons of Interest alerting

Multiple Ports of Entry

Operational data synchronized across locations

Carrier Permit management

Traveler Visa issuance

Flexible business reporting

INTUITIVE AND STREAMLINED

Ategri BioBMS simplifies the Border Control experience for both Officers and Travelers

Building on field learnings gathered over 20 years, the BioBMS system achieves median Traveler processing times of under 90 seconds for Arrivals and sub-60 seconds for Departures – including biometric acquisition.  With efficiencies extending beyond daily operation, the highly-intuitive interface minimizes Officer training requirements (typical system familiarization time is 2-4h) and limits opportunities for data entry errors – improving system accuracy and reducing the need for data review.

DESIGNED FOR EFFICIENCY

Ategri BioBMS is optimized for low-cost, stable operation

Mindful of the cost pressures faced by our customers, Ategri BioBMS is designed for efficient operation and maintenance.  Hosting is local, so cloud costs are avoided; backup processes are fully automated; and commonly-adjusted operational elements of the system can be maintained by a customer’s own Superusers.  And as user requirements evolve, BioBMS’s flexible architecture ensure that new functionalities - such as integrations to other systems – can be provided at highly-competitive cost.
